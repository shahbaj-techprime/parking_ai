What is Parking AI?

Parking AI is an advanced, intelligent parking design solution that automates the creation of highly optimized parking layouts. Simply upload your property or site plan, and our AI-powered system carefully analyzes the available space, access points, and design constraints to generate the most efficient, compliant, and practical parking arrangement.

  • Upload your site plan or property drawing in supported formats
  • AI analyzes space dimensions, driveways, and layout constraints
  • Automatically generates optimized parking layouts in minutes
  • Save days of manual planning and reduce design effort
  • Modify layouts easily with flexible adjustment options
  • Ensure compliance with standard parking rules and guidelines
  • Export professional designs in industry-standard file formats
